Our updated twin air compressor shields are back in stock and ready for immediate delivery Australia-wide.

The latest design now suits the new style of air tank. Each shield is boxed and ready to ship.

The switch, air tank, and hose connector can be mounted on either side, making the setup suitable for left-hand or right-hand mounting. The 4L air tank can also be rotated 90 degrees to suit different hose fitments.

Made from 3mm thick marine-grade aluminium, the shield is strong, lightweight, and will not rust. It includes an easy-to-remove cover panel for access to the air filters, and the bottom mounting bracket can be removed if base mounting is preferred.

The shield can be mounted inside or outside a vehicle or trailer, either vertically or horizontally.
